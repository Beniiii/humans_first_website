REMOTE HEALING

Remote Healing

Remote healing sessions are a simple and logical way of treatment. The same way the digitalisation has changed our every day life so have the treatments changed in the medical world. These days the healings over distance are possible and can be very powerful.

Over the last two years, we have treated a wide range of issues and have seen the treatments were efficient and successful.

How does the remote healing work: A Therapist dials him/herself into the physical energy body on the prearranged time, similar as we receive a phone call. The treatments are defined and arranged in Switzerland and will be executed individually by the therapist in India. The treatments are done while you are sleeping. This way we avoid additional appointments and stress. The quality of the treatments is controlled on a daily basis from Switzerland.

PRANIC HEALING

Pranic Healing

Pranic Healing is a method to increase and strengthen our self-healing processes and aids to reduce illness. It is an effective and no touch energy healing method. It was founded by the Chemical-Engineer and successful businessman Master Choa Kok Sui. After many years of research he developed, documented and tested the various methods to achieve healing, with a wide range of treatments, on physical and psychological issues.

Today this method is taught and practiced in over 90 countries.

Pranic Healing is also taught in some medical education systems and is increasingly practised by doctors as a complement to the classical treatment.

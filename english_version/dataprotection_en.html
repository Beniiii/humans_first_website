Dataprotection

You are important to us:
Therefore, we would like to inform you about data protection and explain our careful handling of it. Humans First Switzerland complies with the provisions of Swiss Data Protection Legislation (DSG). This law is currently under revision. In our privacy policy we go one step further than required today. We are committed to protecting the privacy of all individuals who use our website and to keeping the personal information provided to us by our visitors confidential

Personal Data
We collect data that you voluntarily provide to us, such as: name, first name, address, telephone number and e-mail address within the registration for a treatment or an event. By submitting the form, you consent to the processing of your personal data by Humans First. The collected information will only be used to contact you, to organize your treatment or administration/marketing purposes (e.g. events,). Personal information will not be given to third parties. We do not create personal user profiles of our users.
For statistical purposes, general information (such as statistics on the time distribution for viewing pages) on the access to our website for technical and content review will be stored, aggregated and evaluated anonymously. This data will be kept strictly confidential and will not be disclosed outside Humans First or made available to third parties except as required by applicable law and in particular by the relevant law enforcement agency.

No assurance
Our website was built with utmost care. Nevertheless there can be no assurance for correctedness, accuracy and completeness of the contained information. Contents of the webpage can change without preannouncement.

Links to other websites
Humans First didn’t verify the linked webpages and doesn’t assume any responsibility for their contents, offered products or services or any other part of their components.

Newsletter
Newsletter subscriptions can be cancelled at any time. Information on this can be found on the corresponding newsletters. Addresses of newsletter subscribers will not be forwarded to advertisers and interested parties.

Online Presence in Social Media
We are also present on social networks and platforms such as Facebook in order to communicate with the active customers, prospects and users and to inform them about our services. In the respective networks and platforms, the terms and conditions and the data protection law of the respective operator apply. Facebook is certified under the Privacy Shield Agreement. Thus, the guarantee complies with European data protection law. Data processing by Facebook is handled as part of Facebook's Data Use Policy. You have the right to receive information from us at any time about the information you have stored with us and to arrange for its adaptation, completion or deletion.
